About Us

The Los Angeles Lakers are an American professional basketball team that has been playing since 1947. The Lakers participate in the National Basketball Association, or NBA for short, as members of the league's Western Conference Pacific Division. The Lakers are one of the most successful teams of NBA, and have one 16 NBA championships. Staring from 2015, The Lakers are the second most valuable franchies in the NBA of $2.7 billion according to Forbes, an American magazine business.
